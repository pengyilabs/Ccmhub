Quick answers

How do I add an outlet? +

Use the “New outlet” button in the top bar. Fill in campaign and address, then save.

Can I switch campaigns? +

Yes. Edit an outlet and update the campaign name to reassign reports.

How do I export reports? +

Use the “Download report” action inside an outlet card or detail view.
